World Lore

Majara is a magical world that bases everything off of mana, even the planet is made out of mana.

Each mana type have their own unique properties in uses.

Indepth

White

The Absence Of Mana. You May Be Limited But Arent Weak.

Blue

The Balance of Mana, Perfect For Everyday Tasks (Farming, Cooking, etc).

Red

The Physicality of Mana, Able To Either Enchant Themselves with Strength, Defense, Or Speed.

Green

The Purist of Mana, Divine Healers Are The Purist Of Green.

Purle

The Harvesting of Mana, Some Say It's A Forbidden Magic As It Disrupts The Recycling Of Mana, However Others Argue It Is Essential In Order To Survive.

Yellow

The Embodiment of Mana, Boasting Superior Mana Sensing Abilities. They Are Able To Use Mana Tracking, And Boast Higher Mana Reserves Along With Better Mana Proficiency.

Black

If White Is The Balance of Mana, Black Is The Absorption of Mana. Similar To Harvesting, They're Able To Absorb All Mana Living, Dead, Or Even Inanimate.
